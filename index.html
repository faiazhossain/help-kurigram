Our Plan

We have three major plans of your donation. First we will assure food for a family, then we will assure secure living space and provide medical treatment.

Food

Safe food and water supplies are a major concern following a natural disaster such as flood. People can't cook food because they can't light fire. So we will give them secure food and that's our first concern.

Shelter

As most of the houses are drowned under water so we will make higher shelter using bamboo from the surface. People can take shelter during this hard period.

Treatment

One of the major issue is people get affected of by Water-borne disease. We will provide them needed medicine and also doctor doctors treatment.
